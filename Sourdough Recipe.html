Basic Sourdough Bread

A recipe and steps for a basic sourdough bread

Here are the ingredients you need to make a sourdough bread:

  • Sourdough starter
  • Salt
  • Flour
  • Water

Here are the basic steps:

  1. Autolyse
  2. Combine with sourdough and salt
  3. Bench raise
  4. Folding
  5. First bulk raise
  6. Shaping
  7. Second bulk raise
  8. Bake!
